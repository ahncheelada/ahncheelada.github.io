GroupRead

GroupRead is a web-based collaborative reading environment that focuses on reading comprehension for middle school learners outside the classroom. While observing middle school classrooms, we saw that students often lacked the ability and opportunities to engage in peer discussions that lead to deeper reading comprehension.

GroupRead leverages the affordances of online social communication to encourage dialogue and create a community around reading. It strives to connect readers and engage them in personal, thoughtful, and constructive understandings of text.

User Studies

We did several rounds of user testing, beginning first with paper prototype testing. We then did a analogous experience test.

Using existing technologies, such as Google Docs we could observe how learners might interact in a similar environment.

Version 1

The first version was browser based and included suggested entries into conversations, different levels of interaction with the text and community.

Learners also could interact with personal "mindmap" workspaces and wordbanks for vocabulary storage.

Version 2

The second version became a touch-based interface to afford more casual usage.

It also included a reporting center for teachers and game structures to reward learning.
