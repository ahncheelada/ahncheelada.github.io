RetailMeNot Account User Flow

As the RetailMeNot community continued to grow, plans to make membership a more robust experience highlighted the need to improve account creation and login. The site had historically relied on third party accounts to connect users and did not have a dedicated RetailMeNot sign up.

At the time, user testing revealed that many users were confused and suspicious of logging into RetailMeNot with their personal Facebook and Gmail accounts. It was apparent that a new custom account creation and login system was needed to acquire those users.

Approach

Email newsletter subscription was previously a major area of user engagement but was completely separate from account creation.

We spent some time exploring how we might approach these two and the potential of combining the two user flows

We mapped out the different pathways between logging in and registering for an account. Facebook signup was a priority from a user data perspective,

but requirements included leaving a secondary backdoor for established users to continue using their legacy methods of login.

The final documentation was a map of the different user flows incorporating success and error messaging, landing pages, password retrieval, and email validation.
