Nail Swag

This was a fun project done as part of a hackathon with Alyssa Bernstein and Naomi Umphress. The idea was to create a mobile app that was both a way to discover great nail art and to find out where to have your nails done.

Most online resources, such as Yelp, are organized by salons, but not all salons employ nail artists. Nail Swag, instead, features artists.

Mocks

I worked largely on the visual design. I wanted the app to feel trendy, young, and feminine. We wanted to feature the artists' craft as well as things that inspire them. Emoji and colorful patterns lended to the culture and visual elements of the nail art community.

We also wanted to serve users who just want to enjoy and browse inspiring nail art. Organized by Moods, galleries of user submitted photos are tied together through colorful patterns and textures. We left this work at the concept phase, but future work will include the ability to schedule appointments and submit user reviews.
